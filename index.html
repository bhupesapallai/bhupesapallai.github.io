About Me

I Bhupesa Kumar Pallai,
A proud veteran who brightened my life by serving in the Indian Army, where I performed various challenging projects to strengthen my country, India, and I am the Co-founder and Director of AACSL, an Odisha-based cyber security firm with over two decades of expertise.

As the executive chief of the National Technical Research and Development Committee, I am also a part of IPS Maithili Sharn Gupt's Nation Police Mission, Crime Free Bharat (NTRDC). I have received many decorations and awards, including the General Officer Commendation Card, for his commitment to the nation's responsibility. I am now a Microsoft certified and listed member.

Age is merely a constraint you place on your mind, not a barrier to learning and achieving anything new. After my retirement, I attempted this by obtaining over 200 national and international certificates through an online site. Making a record in the INDIA BOOK OF RECORDS by finishing 200 technical courses between January 30, 2020, and July 28, 2021 is one of my crowning accomplishments.
